◈ ABOUT US

↬ The oldest stationery in Manahari-7, Makawanpur is Manakamana pustak pasal . We have served the people of Manahari for more than two decades. Every year, we supply books to more than 50 schools. We have always attempted to assist Manahari in obtaining an education and will be doing so in the future as well. You can find all kinds of stationery, sports equipment, and toys for kids.
HOPING TO SERVE YOU MORE!!!

◈ WHAT DO WE HAVE TO OFFER?

❖ Stationary Item:

↬ Stationary items are required on a daily basis for work or learning processes. It is available here. We have stationery required for students. Other stationary items like notebooks, registers, tapes, clips, markers, inks, paints, etc.In addition, we print bills, cards, make stamps, and many other things.Contact us for more information.

❖ Sports Items:

↬ We also have sports items like football, football boots, leg pads, volleyball, batminton, table tennis, etc. We are planning to be the best sports centre in Manahari and provide more for people with a passion for sports. We also have medals, trophies, and other special items. Come see us!

❖ Toys:

↬ Toys are enjoyable for children, but what if we could provide a toy that is both enjoyable and educational? We have lots of toys that can teach small children to read and have more intellectual toys.We have got an ABC chart, boards, and study tables. Let's make our children more active and smarter by using toys, not mobile phones.
